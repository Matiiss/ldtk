autolayer This guided tour explains how to create and use Biome enums. Create an Enum that will represent your biomes. Add your possible Biome values (eg. Forest, Desert, Snow etc) You may want to rename your new Enum to something more explicit (eg. Biome). When you're happy with your Enum, open the Level Settings panel. Edit the Level custom fields NOTE: arrays are also supported, you may have multiple Biome enums per level Add a new custom level field Pick the Biome enum you just created. When you're done, open the Layers panel.
